About Me

I’m Salomon Alvarez, a data analyst based in New York City with a background in mechanical engineering and a passion for technology. From the beginning of my career, I was fortunate to work in roles where analyzing data was central to solving problems and driving decisions. That experience sparked a lasting curiosity about how data, math, and computing can come together to uncover insights and create real business impact.

My journey has taken me across industries—from engineering projects to large-scale operations and advertising analytics. At AD+ Advertising, I built SQL workflows, predictive models in Python, and Tableau dashboards that reduced turnaround times, improved quality, and cut costs for thousands of custom projects each year. These projects reinforced my view of data not just as numbers, but as a language for improving efficiency, forecasting demand, and guiding strategy at every level of an organization.

Today, I’m pursuing a Master of Science in Analytics at Georgia Tech, where I’m expanding my expertise in data science, business analytics, and quantitative methods. This program strengthens my ability to connect technology with business outcomes and pushes me to keep growing as a professional.

Outside of work, I enjoy exploring New York City, going to the gym, boxing, and sketching ideas for my next analytics project.
